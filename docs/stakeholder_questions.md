## Stakeholders

- **Service Desk Manager**  
  Responsible for meeting SLA targets and managing ticket backlog.

- **IT Operations Lead**  
  Responsible for queue workload, ticket resolution speed, and team performance.

- **Business Analyst or PMO**  
  Responsible for tracking trends, identifying problem areas, and supporting process improvement.

- **QA or Audit**  
  Responsible for verifying data accuracy and ensuring KPIs are calculated correctly.

## Key Business Questions

1. Are response and resolution SLAs being met for each priority level?
2. Which queues or teams have the highest number of SLA breaches?
3. Is the ticket backlog increasing or decreasing over time?
4. Which ticket categories generate the most tickets and delays?
5. Are tickets being reopened frequently, indicating quality issues?
6. Which queues have longer median resolution times compared to others?
